01 About

Building a Visual Identity Ready for the Next Level

GoZeek is an app that lets kids and grown-ups enjoy exciting and engaging adventures during their museum visits. With curiosity as a starting point, the app encourages kids to connect with the exhibitions in a fun and unpretentious way.

02 Challenge

Designing Around the Brand's Strengths

GoZeek had a bright color palette and original illustrations created by one of the founders, but no system to bring it all together. They needed an identity ready for their website, pitch deck, and app before they could approach museums or investors.

03 Process

A Brand That Kids Actually Wanted to Use

I built the visual identity around what GoZeek does best — making kids feel like real explorers. A bold illustration style, dynamic typography, and a vibrant color palette were combined into a system that works across digital and print touchpoints.

04 Results

The Result:
A Brand the Founders Could Believe In

GoZeek launched with a brand that matched its audience from day one. The new identity gave the founders confidence to pitch for funding and approach museums with materials that looked the part.

Deliverables: Web Design, Pitch Deck, Social Media Graphics, Flyer Design.

01 About

Nordic Track is a one-stop shop for starting a business in Denmark. It combines design, branding, Danish law, and taxes into digital products and service packages. I worked as co-founder and sole designer, building the brand from scratch.

02 Challenge

How do you make starting a business in Denmark feel less intimidating?

Nordic Track needed a brand that could make heavy subjects like taxes and legal structures feel clear and approachable, while still attracting startup founders, freelancers, and international entrepreneurs.

03 Process

Designing a brand that makes business talk feel human

I developed a visual identity using a bold color palette, arrows, and emblems to create energy and movement. The logo was designed with intention: building blocks to represent starting something, and a ladder to signify growth. I also co-wrote and designed the e-book layout, making sure the content felt as clear and approachable as the brand.

04 Branding

Have A Brand Built to Grow With Its Audience

Every asset followed the same visual language, from the website and digital products to social media, print materials, and presentations.

05 Results

The result: A Brand Built for everyday founders

Nordic Track launched with a full brand and an e-book ready to go. The first events ran in partnership with the local Kommune, bringing practical guidance to people who wanted to start a business without the pressure of becoming the next big startup.

Deliverables: Logo and Visual Identity, Website, E-book Layout, Digital Assets, Print Materials, Presentations.

01 About
02 Challenge

04 Process

01 About

EcoFabrics is a concept B2B platform for sustainable textile sourcing. It connects fashion brands and procurement teams with certified, traceable fabric suppliers. The project is self-initiated, built to address a real gap in the sustainable textile market.

02 Challenge

How do you help B2B buyers trust a supplier in under five minutes?

B2B buyers face the same problem every time they search for a sustainable fabric supplier: greenwashing, missing certifications, and buried technical information. EcoFabrics needed a platform built around speed and transparency.

03 Research

Designing for Skeptical Buyers

I ran a secondary research review on B2B sustainable textile sourcing trends (2024–2025). Four findings shaped every design decision: certification is a dealbreaker, speed determines the outcome, greenwashing is the main trust barrier, and EU compliance pressure makes traceability a buying requirement.

04 Process

Different Personas, Same Need: Fast Access to Trusted Information

Sofia, 34, is a Product Developer. She researches thoroughly before contacting any supplier. She checks certifications first, specs second, and origin third. She is skeptical of vague claims.

Lars, 42, is a Procurement Manager. He evaluates multiple suppliers at once and builds shortlists for compliance review. He filters fast and decides whether to request a sample within minutes. He does not read long content and will not email a supplier for basic information.

05 Results

Every Structure Serves a User Need

Certification badges appear above the fold. A persistent filter system covers fiber type, certification, and origin. The primary CTA is Sample Request. Spec sheets are downloadable directly, with no email required. Sustainability content lives in dedicated pages, keeping product pages focused on specs.

Deliverables: Research Brief, User Personas, Sitemap, User Flows, Wireframe Descriptions, UI Design.
